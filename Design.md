# Design Guidelines for Lumina

## Design 
An asthetic interface with buttons controls and the logo on the top left.

## Brand Identity
* **Brand Name:** Lumina
* **Aesthetic Direction:** Warm, minimal, elegant, and nature-inspired. It feels like a high-end, cozy boutique shop.
* **Visual Roots:** The design directly translates the elements of the company logo: a fine circular frame, crisp structural layout lines, and organic leaf details.

---

## Color Palette Guide

To maintain complete visual harmony with the brand logo, use these specific color tones across all website pages and sections:

* **Main Background (Light Cream):** A soft, warm cream color used across all large section backdrops instead of stark white. It makes the entire website feel inviting, premium, and easy on the eyes.
* **Primary Color (Copper-Bronze):** A rich, warm brown-gold tone. This is assigned to major text headers, thin decorative line breaks, and important action items.
* **Secondary Color (Sage Green):** A soft, natural leaf green. This is used exclusively to highlight special details, active links, or organic product tags.
* **Accent Color (Toasted Almond):** A warm, sandy beige tone. This is reserved for smaller highlights or as the color change when a user hovers over a primary button.
* **Text Color (Deep Charcoal):** A soft, dark matte grey instead of pure black. This keeps the long paragraphs highly readable while maintaining a premium look.

---

## Website Layout and Design Rules

### 1. Page Layout and Spacing
* **Generous Spacing:** Keep the website sections feeling open, spacious, and clean. Use plenty of empty space around product images and blocks of text so the items look high-end.
* **Fine Framing:** Use thin, delicate layout lines to separate different sections of the page, mimicking the fine line-art detailing from the logo circular frame.

### 2. Typography Strategy
* **Main Headings:** Use a bold, elegant, classic font with clean edges for the big titles to match the structural weight of the word LUMINA in the logo.
* **Subtitles and Quotes:** Use a flowing, elegant cursive or italic text style to mimic the artistic Handmade script.
* **Paragraphs and Shopping Details:** Use a simple, clean, modern standard font that is incredibly easy to read on both mobile phones and desktop computers.

### 3. Interactive Components and Buttons
* **Main Action Buttons:** Items like Buy Now or Add to Cart must have a solid Copper-Bronze background with white text. When a customer hovers their mouse over them, the button should smoothly change to the warm Toasted Almond color.
* **Secondary Action Buttons:** Items like Learn More or View Details must be clear with a thin Copper-Bronze outline.
* **Product Highlighting Labels:** Use tiny Sage Green background tags sitting on top of product images to cleanly call out premium features like Wooden Wick, Hand-Poured, or Organic.

## Structure Guidelines for Google Stitch

* **Header Section:** Centered brand logo with clean, spread-out navigation links underneath.
* **Hero Section:** A large welcome banner showing a candle lit in a cozy room, flanked by a bold classic heading and a script font tagline.
* **Product Grid Section:** A clean three-column grid system displaying candle jars with their respective names, pricing, and small green highlight tags.
* **Story Section:** A warm cream background block detailing the artisanal process behind the candles, framed by thin bronze lines.
