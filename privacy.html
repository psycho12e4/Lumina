Privacy Policy

We keep things simple: the only personal information we collect is what you share with us directly — your email address when you subscribe to our newsletter, or your contact details when you reach out through our contact form.

We never sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time, and you may ask us to delete any information we hold about you by writing to hello@luminacandles.com.

This site does not use advertising trackers or analytics cookies. Your shopping bag is stored only on your own device.
